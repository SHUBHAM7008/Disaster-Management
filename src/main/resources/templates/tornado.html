Tornado Precautions

Seek Shelter Immediately

As soon as a tornado warning is issued, immediately take shelter in a basement or interior room on the lowest floor away from windows.

Avoid Windows

Keep away from windows, as they can shatter during a tornado. If you cannot find a basement, get into a small, windowless interior room.

Cover Yourself

Use heavy blankets, mattresses, or helmets to protect your head and neck. If no materials are available, crouch down and cover your head with your arms.

Monitor Weather Updates

Stay informed with the latest tornado warnings by listening to weather radios or using mobile apps that provide real-time alerts.
