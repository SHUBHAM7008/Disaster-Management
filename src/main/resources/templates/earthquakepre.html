Earthquake Precautions

Drop, Cover, and Hold On

Drop to your hands and knees, cover your head and neck, and hold on until the shaking stops. Stay indoors and away from windows.

Stay Away from Windows

Windows can shatter during an earthquake, so it is important to stay away from glass and windows during the shaking.

Find Safe Shelter

During shaking, take shelter under sturdy furniture like a table or desk. If no furniture is available, crouch in an interior corner and protect your head.

Prepare an Emergency Kit

Keep an emergency kit with essential supplies like water, food, a flashlight, and a first-aid kit. Be sure to have enough supplies for at least three days.
