Flood Precautions

Move to Higher Ground

In case of flooding, move to higher ground immediately. Avoid walking through floodwaters as they can be deeper than they appear.

Avoid Driving

Never drive through flooded areas. Water can easily sweep away a vehicle, and roads may be washed out or hidden under water.

Prepare an Emergency Kit

Pack an emergency kit with essentials such as water, food, medication, important documents, and flashlights. Ensure it is accessible.

Stay Informed

Keep updated on flood alerts and warnings through radio, mobile apps, or official government sources. Flooding can escalate quickly.
